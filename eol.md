# End of life

The following products are end of life, replaced by the [new consolidated tooling](/commands):

- Structurizr cloud service (no replacement)
- Structurizr Lite (replaced by [local](/local))
- Structurizr CLI (replaced by [pull](/pull), [push](/push), [export](/export), etc)
- Structurizr on-premises installation (replaced by [server](/server))

See [Introducing Structurizr vNext](https://www.patreon.com/posts/146923136) for more details.
